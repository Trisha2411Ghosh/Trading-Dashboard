Updated API ENDPOINTS
----------------------------
For all stocks - 
postgre : https://trading-dashboard-backend.onrender.com/stocks?dbsource=postgres
clickhouse : https://trading-dashboard-backend.onrender.com/stocks?dbsource=clickhouse

for specific stock - 
postgre - https://trading-dashboard-backend.onrender.com/stocks/{symbol}?dbsource=postgres
clickhouse - https://trading-dashboard-backend.onrender.com/stocks/{symbol}?dbsource=clickhouse

for specific trade -
postgre - https://trading-dashboard-backend.onrender.com/trades/{symbol}?dbsource=postgres
clickhouse - https://trading-dashboard-backend.onrender.com/trades/{symbol}?dbsource=clickhouse

for specific instrument -
postgre - https://trading-dashboard-backend.onrender.com/instruments/{symbol}?dbsource=postgres
clickhouse - https://trading-dashboard-backend.onrender.com/instruments/{symbol}?dbsource=clickhouse

for combined data -
postgre - https://trading-dashboard-backend.onrender.com/combined/{symbol}?dbsource=postgres
clickhouse - https://trading-dashboard-backend.onrender.com/combined/{symbol}?dbsource=clickhouse
